As you may or may not know Pac-Man has Power Berries that he gobbles up to gain new Powers here are some of them


1)Ice Berrie

This power up gives Pac-Man the ability to blow ice at his enimies and freeze them. Ghosts too can get frozen if Pac-Man uses his power up on them.



2)Elder Berry

This power up gives Pac-Man the ability to have more strength, climb buildings, and transform into an ape. Sort of like King Kong.



3)Titanium Berry



This power up gives Pac some magnetic powers. He acts literally just like a magnet. This Power up also makes him get a cool metalic silver color and red and blue boxing gloves and shoes.
